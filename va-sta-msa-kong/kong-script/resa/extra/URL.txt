valid URL with key-auth:
==============
curl http://xyz.mycompany.fun:8000/session-api/public/session --header "apikey: consumer1-key"
curl http://xyz.mycompany.fun:8000/session-api/public/session?apiKey=consumer1-key

invalid URL without key-auth:
==============
curl http://xyz.mycompany.fun:8000/session-api/public/session
